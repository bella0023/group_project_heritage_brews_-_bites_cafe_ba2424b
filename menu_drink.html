Beverage Heritage


Vanilla Latte

A velvety blend of rich espresso and steamed milk, infused with the sweet and comforting essence of vanilla. Indulge in the perfect harmony of bold coffee and smooth, aromatic vanilla in every sip.


Dirty Chai Latte

A bold fusion of spiced chai tea and robust espresso, creating a flavorful and invigorating beverage. Experience the delightful marriage of aromatic spices and the kick of espresso, perfectly balanced with steamed milk for a uniquely energizing treat.


Triple Chocolate Hot Cocoa

A decadent blend of dark, milk, and white chocolates creates a heavenly and comforting elixir. Indulge in the richness of this triple chocolate delight with every comforting sip.


Tiramisu Latte

An exquisite blend of velvety espresso, steamed milk, and the indulgent flavors of mascarpone and cocoa. Sip on the creamy richness reminiscent of the classic Italian dessert, creating a delightful harmony of coffee and dessert in every latte.

Iced Hojicha Latte

A cool fusion of roasted green tea, steamed milk, and ice, delivering an invigorating and subtly sweet iced latte experience with unique toasty notes.


Dalgona Iced Coffee

A trendy delight with a whipped coffee cloud crowning cold milk and ice. Crafted from instant coffee, sugar, and hot water, this sweet, frothy treat brings a cool and flavorful twist to your coffee experience. Sip into the trendiness!


Coffee Jelly Dessert

A delightful concoction of coffee-infused gelatin, creating a bouncy and refreshing treat. Immerse yourself in the rich, aromatic coffee flavors harmoniously set within the cool, jiggly texture of the jelly.


Chocolate Coffee Date Shake

Indulge in a blend where the richness of chocolate and the boldness of coffee meet the natural sweetness of dates. This shake is a delightful fusion of flavors and textures, offering a velvety, satisfying experience in every sip.
